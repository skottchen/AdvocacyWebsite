What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy refers to the ability to use and understand digital technologies effectively, critically, and responsibly. Digital literacy is crucial is today‚Äôs technology-driven society as it enables individuals to access information, communicate effectively, and participate fully in the digital economy.

Impact!

Our digital literacy skills can affect the decisions we make everyday at home, work, and everywhere we go.

People with low levels of digital literacy are more likely to believe misinformation they see online and then implement that misinformation into their own lives.

Our digital literacy skills can affect our interactions with others.

A significant amount of our interactions these days are online. People with high digital literacy skills are more likely to be respectful to others online as well as think critically about what others say and post online.
